Yuzhen Qin

Radboud University, Nijmegen, Netherlands.

Office: MM 03.385

Email: yuzhen.qin@ru.nl

I am a Tenure-Track Assistant Professor in the Department of Machine Learning and Neural Computing in Donders Institute for Brain, Cognition and Behaviour at Radboud University, the Netherlands. I am also a member of Dutch Brain Interface Initiative (DBI2), a multi-institude consortium backed by a substantial 10-year grant totaling € 21.9M.

I obtained my Ph.D. in Dec. 2019 from University of Groningen, the Netherlands, advised by Prof. Ming Cao. I received my M.E. from Wuhan University in 2015. From 2020 to 2023, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher with Prof. Fabio Pasqualetti in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the University of California, Riverside.

Curent Research Topics

  • Modeling and Control of Brain Dynamics
  • Efficient Online Decision-Making (Reinforcement Learning and Bandits)
